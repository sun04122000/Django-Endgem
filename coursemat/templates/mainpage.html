Welcome to Igem!

The Doc&Tori Foundation has this time come up with an app that provides a well structured way to properly maintain and store your complete course material. This app is user-friendly app with unique feature of uploading your course content subject wise.Besides this it contains no advertisements so as to provide utmost convenience to the user. Found in 2020 by Doc & Tori, Igem/Endgem has come up a long way from its beginning in the Pandemic.
The passion for helping students to manage their academics along with their busy schedules gave them the impetus to turn their hard work and inspiration into to a booming online virtual storage app.
We now serve customers all over India, and are thrilled to be a part of the accessable wing of the education industry.
We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.
